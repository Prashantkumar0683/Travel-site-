Welcome to Hvar

Hvar is a beautiful island in Croatia, located in the Adriatic Sea. Known for its crystal-clear waters, vibrant nightlife, and historical sites, Hvar is a must-visit destination for travelers looking for both relaxation and adventure.

Things to Do in Hvar

Relax at the Beaches

Enjoy Hvar's pristine beaches, from sandy coves to rocky shores, perfect for swimming, sunbathing, and water sports.

Explore Nature

Hike or bike through Hvar's lush landscapes, including pine forests and hills with panoramic views of the island and sea.

Experience Nightlife

Hvar is famous for its vibrant nightlife. From beach bars to luxurious clubs, the island offers an exciting party scene.

How to Get to Hvar

Hvar is easily accessible by ferry from Split, or by private yacht. Here are your best travel options:

By Ferry

The ferry from Split to Hvar takes around 1.5 hours. Multiple daily services make it convenient for visitors arriving by boat.

By Private Yacht

For a more luxurious experience, you can charter a private yacht and sail directly to Hvar from Split or other coastal cities.

Best Time to Visit Hvar

The best time to visit Hvar is during the spring and autumn months (April to June, September to October) when the weather is pleasant, and the island is less crowded.
